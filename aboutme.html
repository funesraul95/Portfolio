Hi. I'm Raul Funes.

I am an undergrad at the College of Westchester in White Plains, New York. My goal in schooling is to become a 3D game artist. I have been passionate about the beauty in video games since I was first handed a SNES controller at the age of three. I thrive in creative and collaborative environments where hard work and passion for making great games is valued. I am inspired by horror, plat-forming, and adventure genre based games and by the likes of game development companies such as Capcom, Epic, Insomniac, Ubisoft, DICE, Crytek, Rockstar, Naughty Dog, Rocksteady, and Visceral to get a feel of the type of games Iâ€™d like to develop for.

Coming into The College of Westchester I really had no interest in art and just had a set vision on designing 3D models, however my time in this institute has caused me to grasp an interest in art. I have only been drawing for about a year, however I have improved greatly since and plan to keep improving for pleasure, make personal T-shirts for myself and others, and to only better my skills at my true passion. I plan on continuing my studies for game design at Rochester Institute of Technology or The University of Arts in London if given the opportunity.
